Description

A very simple Settings theme that uses Bubblegum theme color palette (thanks to itsCrx). Almost all settings icons are themed.

It has 6 optional Switchknob themes: Blue, Green, Yellow, Orange, Red, and Purple.

It looks great on light, dark, or true black backgrounds. Just take a look at the screenshots!
