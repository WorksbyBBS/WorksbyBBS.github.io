Description

Lineal Music Controls. They work in the music app, LS, and CC.

These have been tested on iPhone X running iOS 11.3.1, and 12.1.1 on iPhone XS, and iOS 13.2.3 on 11 Pro. They are @3x, so I assume they'll only work on X/Plus devices.

Important!

You will need Snowboard + UI Extension from SparkDev repo, or iThemer.

I recommend Snowboard + UI Exention for best results.
